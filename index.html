About

Republic Music is a unique new label and collective based in New York that connects producers, DJs and fans worldwide in the spirit of the underground.

From Brooklyn to the UK, Spain to Sydney and beyond, Republic aims to elevate the standard and get heads nodding in every hemisphere with highly advanced dance music that is grounded in the old school but looks to the next level.

The unifying principle of deep house – the ageless, timeless, all-encompassing sound of real house music – is the foundation of Republic's musical philosophy. However our roster of artists are inspired by and incorporate a rich kaleidoscope of contemporary sounds, including funk, garage, indie, jazz, Latin and electronica. Soul is the common element, and soul is found in all forms of music as long as heads put their heart in the mix. Not limited to one genre or tempo, Republic’s mission is to dish out the soul of the future. This diverse melting pot of grooves is the real “world music.” It’s a masterful fusion of acoustic and electronic; it’s dark as midnight and light as the promise of a new day; it’s just for a good time and it’ll save your life.

Republic stands for rebellion against the mainstream, against cynicism, against the same old way of doing things, against commercial noise. We walk a true path, keeping hope alive for a better time – a time when people realize they are already free, to be gathered under the sway of a universal soundtrack with power to soothe, uplift and heal.

Republic means unity in diversity and freedom in love. It also means kick-ass funky soul with zero bullshit. It’s more than a label – it’s a passion, a movement and a way of life. All with open minds are welcome. Come, join the movement, and take your first step into a larger musical world.

Neil Smallridge

facebooktwittersoundcloud

Hailing from Manchester, where he holds down a number of weekly DJ residencies, and a veteran of Ibiza's legendary Pacha, Neil Smallridge is well known for his deep-house, garage and funk grooves which have kept discerning music heads rocking east and west for years.

This well-travelled selector’s way with soulful sounds past, present and future has resulted in some globally attuned, musically diverse and – of course – smoking-hot jams.

Neil currently resides at Manchester hotspot The Blackdog Ballroom, where he plays a broad spectrum of house, disco, classics, indie, funk, hip hop and soul to a full house in an epic eight-hour set every week. With no shortage of flavors, sounds and inspiration, it is little wonder why he draws such huge and appreciative crowds.

Max Martinez

facebooksoundcloud

Max has made some big noise on the East Coast scene with a string of steady-rocking remixes and originals for Maya Records, Aztec Tribalism, Selecta Recordings and others. The repertoire is quickly growing for this rising star, whose sound people can't seem to get enough of.

Drawing inspiration from a rich musical background of hiphop, freestyle, house and funk, Max's releases are instant classics underscored by moody keys, floor-stomping beats and dubbed-out soul, evoking the spirit of New York's deeply rooted dance culture.

Though he currently calls Pennsylvania home, Max’s native Brooklyn roots are evident in his production style and flexibility. When he's not cranking out some of the sickest beats this side of the Atlantic, Max can be found tearing up dancefloors throughout the Tri-State area within a growing sphere of influence as more people discover his amazing talent.

Seph a.k.a. Dreamer's Dub

facebooksoundcloud

Seph has been a champion of the phenomenon known as house music all his life: a fan, collector, historian and archivist since the music’s earliest days, and now one of New York’s most eloquent ambassadors of the real underground sound.

Appearing on several labels, from House Café Music to Maya Records, he produces and remixes under the names Dreamer’s Dub and Valentin (with Maya’s DJ Joeski), stacking up a collection of expressive originals and otherwordly remixes featuring his penchant for emotive synths, smoking drumkits and sexy, sprawling basslines.

Inspired by the demand for this innovative sound, Seph continues to create, promote and tour from his hometown, spreading the message of house on many fronts. Whether rocking crowds worldwide, organizing memorable events or making beats in the studio, Seph is constantly working to redefine and shape what people think of real house music.

Steven Garcia

facebooksoundcloud

Steven’s mastery of dynamic production and evolved compositional sensibility are familiar to househeads on both sides of the Atlantic, thanks in part to standout tracks on compilations from Café del Mar and Turntables on the Hudson which have enjoyed global exposure.

The soundtrack Steven cultivates is driven by a lifetime passion for keyboards, percussion and record collecting. Though best known for his dancefloor offerings, Mr Garcia habitually produces a connoisseur’s secret stash of funk, soul and electronica with tremendous dexterity.

This former Brooklynite currently lives in Spain, where he regularly wows audiences with sessions that serve as homages to the prolific rhythm of his muse. Both on and off the decks Steven establishes a stratospheric standard, pushing the boundaries of sonic possibility in a way that can only be described as artistic genius.

DJ Ino

facebooksoundcloud

Ino has contributed much to the electronic scene in the last decade. With efforts spanning several albums, collaborations and the launch of his own label House Café Music, he has put his hometown of La Coruña on the map by pioneering at the forefront of house music in Spain.

Among the impressive roster of artists and house legends which have appeared on his label, Ino's work stands out. His discography has left an indelible mark on a healthy collection of genres – mainly house, but also jazz, disco, and even a full-length hip-hop album – with a sound that is unmistakably his own.

Ino currently holds events at La Terrazza, a space located in the five-star Hotel Finisterre that has managed to position itself as one of the most successful events in the city of La Coruña. He also runs Victoria Club from the hotel's massive and decadent ballroom, where a packed house gathers every month to hear the top-notch sound which has become the signature of this Iberian visionary.

Pete Moss

facebooksoundcloud

Following his beginnings, providing soundtracks for sunrises up and down the East Coast, Pete Moss settled himself down in Philadelphia, a city with a warm reception to the soulful sounds of his selections.

Having gathered equipment along the way, he focused intently on production and gained encouragement and inspiration form the musical clan that existed in Philly which included the likes of King Britt, Josh Wink and the whole Ovum Records cast and crew. Pete has developed into one of dance music's finest talents and reminds us constantly with his late-night sets and impeccably soulful production skills.

His present releases are staples for the community that no self-respecting DJ would be caught without. Prolific and hard-working, Pete has served up a steady stream of music despite his hectic travel schedule that has taken him around the world more times than he can count. This year proves another hot one for Pete, with upcoming releases on Bananza, Nordic trax, his own Recline imprint, Sound Between Movement and Plastic City; and his much-awaited second full-length album on the way.

4 Da People

myspacefacebooksoundcloud

Michael Santos (AKA Mikie Wilde) brings us a worldly perspective on dance music. This former New Jersey native brought a taste of America back with him to Portugal, where he began his musical career as a band musician. In 1997 he started to play professionally in several clubs and cult bars in Lisbon where his unique sound was met with much critical acclaim.

In 2006 and 2007 his musical influence spread to France where he played in several clubs in Paris and Bordeaux. In 2007 he came back to Portugal to lock down a residency at Trumps Club, one of the best LGBT club in Lisbon, and continues to hold monthly residences in several clubs throughout the city.

Michael maintains a productive pace in the studio, working under various aliases such as Erotik Mike, The Pusherman and, most recently, 4 Da People. His sound is very fresh and original yet puts the listener right at home. While definitely giving a nod to classic house anthems, this artist blazes his own sonic trail with a patent blend of hypnotic synths, emotional basslines and simple, yet effective percussion. His agility both in the mix and on a record is what distinguishes him from his peers, placing him among Europe’s eminent luminaries of authentic house music.
